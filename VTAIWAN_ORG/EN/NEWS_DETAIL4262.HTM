Online conference Part 7 titled "Sharing Economy" is scheduled for August 27th

 

The 7th online conference for the Virtual World Law Adjustment Program will be held on Thursday, August 27th at 7 pm. This conference will be podcast live!

 

The "Sharing Economy" which has shown a vigorous development in recent years is the main discussion topic for this conference. This new business model will have a severe impact on the traditional legal system. For instance, Uber and Airbnb pose a challenge to legal concepts pertaining to the transportation and tourism sectors, respectively. The conference discussions will focus on Uber and transportation laws. Representatives of the Ministry of Transportation and Communications and Uber have been invited to deliver reports at the conference. Prior to the conference, views and opinions of the general public regarding this issue have been collected via the polis questionnaire system. We welcome everyone interested parties to browse the results of this poll and provide data analysis reports.

We welcome online participation from all parties interested in developments of the virtual world.
