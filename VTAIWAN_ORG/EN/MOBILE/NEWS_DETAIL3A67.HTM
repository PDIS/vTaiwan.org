Announcements2015-01-30

You are invited to take part in the discussions happening on the Virtual World Law Adjustment Forum!

We established the “vTaiwan Forum (https://vtaiwan.tw/#!/)” to compile opinions from all walks of life as we carry out virtual world law adjustments. This helps us monitor law adjustment needs and decide the direction of law adjustments.

The call for opinions within the Forum is led by the relevant supervisory authorities. This information will be compiled as a reference for supervisory authorities when drafting policies.

In accordance with the program timeline, “crowd-funding” and “closed corporations” have been established as current discussion topics.

Come and join the discussion!
