Ministry Special Report and Presentation on the Upcoming Virtual World Law Adjustment Online conference

The Virtual World Law Adjustment Online conference is about to take place on March 12th, from 7 to 9 PM. There will be two focuses to the discussion this time: “closed corporations” and “taxation of online transactions”. The supervisory ministry is providing a special report and presentation for all interested parties to peruse before the event, to facilitate discussions at the conference.

 

We invite you to take part in the ongoing discussions on the vTaiwan Online Law Discussion Platform (https://vtaiwan.tw/) to voice your valuable opinions on law adjustments and policy amendments with regard to “closed corporations” and “taxation of online transactions”. Any posts made before the start of the conference on March 12th may be included in conference discussions.
