The National Development Council invites you to take part in the creation of the “White Paper on Smart Internet for a New Taiwan”

The Executive Yuan is now planning a draft of the “White Paper on Smart Internet for a New Taiwan” as a future government blueprint to implement information services and set the direction for Taiwan’s future internet applications. There will be 5 main aspects to the white paper: “basic environment”, “transparent governance”, “smart living”, “internet economy”, and “intellectual national territory”. Various ministries are now utilizing online discussions and communication via in-person conferences to call for opinions from all parties. The initial draft of the white paper is now near completion. The National Development Council sincerely invites you to visit the Public Policy Network Participation Platform (http://join.gov.tw) to take part in this discussion, so that the content of this action plan conforms to future public expectations. Thank you!
