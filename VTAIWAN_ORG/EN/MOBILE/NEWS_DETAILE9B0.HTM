Online conference Part 9 titled "De-identification of Personal Information" is scheduled for February 23rd

 

The 9th online conference for the Virtual World Law Adjustment Program will be held at the Executive Yuan on February 23rd, 2016 at 7 pm. This conference will be podcast live on LivehouseIn! (Please visit the Platform’s latest news for updates on conference times)

 

The “de-identification of personal information” is the main discussion topic for this conference. Taiwan leapt from its global open data ranking of 11th place to 1st place in 2015. At the same time, partial amendments to the Personal Information Protection Act were also passed on December 30th, 2015. How can we maintain the right to privacy despite these open data trends? The conference discussions will focus on the methods and risks involved in personal information de-identification. Relevant ministries, scholars, experts, social network participants and the Executive Yuan’s Youth Advisory Group have been invited to take part in the conference. Views and opinions of the general public regarding this issue are now being collected via the polis questionnaire system. We invite all interested parties to browse this poll and share your opinions with us.
